## Introduce
Hello everyone, and welcome to my blog.

I'm currently a third-year Cybersecurity student, preparing to enter my final year at university. Right now, I'm enjoying my summer break while waiting for the results of my internship applications, which I recently submitted.
During this period, I've realized that I'm not as confident in my skills and knowledge as I would like to be. Like many students approaching graduation, I've been thinking a lot about my future career, finding a job, and completing my graduation project. These concerns motivated me to make better use of my time instead of simply waiting.
